Joint Angel

Web App

A self-assessment tool to track recovery from joint surgery. We were involved from ideation through to design and development of the entire product.

In conjunction with Imperial College London, Joint Angel is a new venture which provides a place for patients to monitor and track their joint recovery. We were involved from ideation through to brand and ultimately full build. Following consultation with users, the decision was made to optimise the app for tablet use and scale up to desktop.
